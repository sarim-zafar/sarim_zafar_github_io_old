Timeline

  • Needfinding

    19th of February

    We interviewed some people to figure out what is the problem in current university admission tests.We merged our findings for this part below into POV!

  • POVs and HMWs

    19th of March

    After interviewing a few more people, we sat down and analysed our findings, and came up with several point of views (POV) that consumers might have, and figured out how might we (HMW) address them. You can see our findings here!

  • Concept Video

    19th of April

    In order to convey the value our idea would provide to a user, we made a concept video that depicts how we envision a day in the life of a user. Check out the accompanying slides (here)

  • Low Fi protoype

    7th of May

    We put our idea to the test by creating rough mockups of what the eventual product would look like, and simulated the experience of using the app with test users. The results told us a lot. To access the report we curated to for our low-fi prototype, here

  • Medium-Fi Prototype

    May 25th

    With the feedback we received from our low-fi prototype, we decided that a revamp of the UI interface was in order. Checkout the med-fi prototype here.

  • Heuristic Evaluation

    After our revamp of the concept, we put out medium-fi prototype through a rigorous heuristic evalution. You can see the results here.

  • Hi-Fi Prototype

    Our work all culminated into this one Hi-Fidelity prototype. Try it out!

  • Poster, pitch slides & final report

    To present our work to the judges we made this poster. You also might be interested in seeing the slide(pdf) we used to pitch our idea to the judges. We'll throw in our final report too.
